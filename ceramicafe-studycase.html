CeramiCafe, Del Mar

Marketing · Research · Content Strategy

CeramiCafe is a ceramics painting studio in Del Mar, San Diego. This project was focused on improving their digital presence and helping them better connect with their audience through more cohesive, creative, and engaging content.

What I Did

  • Audited Instagram, TikTok, and website to identify gaps in branding and social media presence
  • Conducted competitive analysis to find growth opportunities and position strategies
  • Created user personas to guide content direction based on consumer behaviors and platform trends
  • Researched and recommended micro-influencers that align with the brand’s audience and values

Tools Used

Instagram, Meta Ads, TikTok, Yelp, Excel

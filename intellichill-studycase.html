Intellichill – Smart Fridge App

UX/UI Design · Product Strategy · Usability Testing

Intellichill is a smart fridge app prototype designed to help users reduce food waste, manage their groceries, and discover recipe ideas based on ingredients they already have. Built with a focus on intuitive interaction and sustainability, the prototype explores how AI and UX can work together to improve everyday life.

What I Did

  • Developed concept and built prototype collaboratively with my team
  • Designed features including expiration alerts, live fridge cam, and recipe suggestions using AI
  • Applied UX and HCI principles such as Nielsen’s heuristics to streamline navigation and usability
  • Led usability testing that resulted in a 27% reduction in task completion time for managing fridge inventory

Tools Used

Figma, Adobe Creative Cloud
